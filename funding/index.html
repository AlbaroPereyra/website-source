Donations

Donations to the Ebean ORM project are greatly appreciated. These donations help fund ongoing maintenance and upkeep of the project.

 

Pay it forward

The Ebean project and it's users lean on other open source projects for our success. As such Ebean has a Pay it forward policy that means 10 percentage of donations to this project are paid forward on an annual basis.

10% of donations will be paid forward to:

  • H2 database
  • Jackson
